why did we explore this?

  • data-driven:

    we live in the age of data, so we should be comfortable working with it (look at “how design needs data” by joshua noble)

  • 3d:

    data viz is largely 2d graphs-and-charts, and people grown numb to it over time because of how much they are exposed to it.

  • interactive:

    more memorable. possible to tell a narrative, by showing different things in sequence.

  • objects:

    things that are more relatable can potentially be more memorable

  • web:

    can be distributed en masse, instead of being niche (like AR/VR), or requiring special apps.
